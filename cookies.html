Cookie Policy

Last updated: 20 July 2026

Essential storage

This site stores your cookie preference in your browser so the consent banner does not repeatedly appear. This is necessary for the preference tool to work.

Optional analytics

The downloadable build does not include third-party analytics by default. If analytics are added later, they should remain disabled until the visitor accepts optional cookies.

Media and external links

The experience contains a locally hosted intro video and links to external ClientCapture sites. Those external sites may apply their own cookie policies.

Managing preferences

Use the “Cookie settings” control in the site footer to reopen the preference banner. You can also clear site data through your browser settings.
